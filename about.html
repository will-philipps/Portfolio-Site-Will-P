About

I’m a UI/UX designer, with a creative agency and tech education background.

I have experience brand building, developing marketing campaigns and managing project delivery teams. Drawn to work that uses creativity to bring products to life, my career has taken me from content writing and editing a magazine in Beijing, to marketing for some of the world's biggest brands, and helping a tech education organisation grow its brand and business in the UK.

I now specialise in UI/UX and product design. Storytelling, branding and collaborating on brilliant products form the unifying thread in my career – skills and experience I carry across into my approach to design.

I’m available for full-time work – preferably office-based – as well as freelance work. You can get in touch with me here.
